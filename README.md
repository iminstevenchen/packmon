# Packmon — Your Travel Packing Partner

**Packmon** builds a personalized packing list from your destination, trip length, and travel style — and flags customs restrictions, airline-security limits, and local etiquette *before* you leave. Pack what you need; avoid what you can't bring.

## The problem

Generic packing checklists give everyone the same list and stop at "toothbrush, charger." They don't know your destination's customs rules, airline-security limits, or local culture — so travelers still get stopped at the border for something they didn't know they couldn't bring.

## What Packmon does

**🧳 Packs the right things** — A checklist generated for *this* trip: tailored to destination and weather, adjusted for travel style, with a clear reason attached to every item.

**🛂 Clears the border with confidence** — Customs rules shown as **Prohibited / Permit required / Must declare**, each with the reason, official source, and last-verified date — so a rule becomes an action, not just a warning.

**🌏 Knows the destination, not just the checklist** — Airline-security limits and city-specific culture and etiquette, all in one place before departure.

**🙂 Company for solo travelers** — "Packmon Jelly" tracks packing progress, shares practical travel trivia, and offers encouragement along the way.

## Personalized onboarding

Before generating a list, Packmon asks a few questions — age group, travel companions, prior travel experience, and packing style (light vs. thorough) — so the checklist is genuinely yours rather than a one-size-fits-all template.

## Home-screen widgets

Two widget sizes surface departure date, destination, and live packing progress right on the home screen — a nudge toward "ready to go" every time you pick up your phone.

## Design belief

> "Simple can be harder than complex."

Real simplicity means understanding all the complexity *for* the traveler first — folding scattered packing info, customs rules, and travel reminders into steps that are easy to read and easy to act on.
